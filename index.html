Your Challenge: Create a Profile Card

Your task is to replace this page with your own personal profile card. Follow the steps below.

Instructions

  • 1. Clear the Page

    Delete everything inside the <body> of the index.html file.

  • 2. Add Your Card HTML

    You have to make a card in html inside <body>. Make sure to replace the bracketed content with your own information. The card must contain
    Your Name ,
    At least two things you like. 

    <div class="card">
    <h2>[Your Name]</h2>
    <ul>
        <li>I like: [First Thing You Like]</li>
        <li>I also like: [Second Thing You Like]</li>
    </ul>
</div>

  • 3. Add Your CSS

    Add the any CSS of your choice personalize your card and (try not to make changes to any other cards) style.css to style your card. 

    body {
    background-color: #f0f0f0;
}
.card {
    background-color: white;
    border: 1px solid #ccc;
    border-radius: 8px;
    padding: 20px;
    margin: 50px;
    width: 300px;
}

  • 4. Commit

    Commit your changes with a suitable commit message, for example "Add profile card". DO NOT PUSH YET!

What's Next?

After you have pushed your changes, there will be a new commit in the repository from the instructor. Pull those changes to get your final assignment: resolving a merge conflict.

Good luck!
